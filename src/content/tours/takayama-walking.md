  ## About This Tour

  This half-day guided tour is ideal if you are:

  - Unsure what to see in the Takayama area
  - Keen to learn more about Takayama's history and culture
  - Working with limited time and want to make the most of it

  The tour runs for 3.5 hours, visiting Takayama's must-see highlights: the morning market, traditional merchant houses, the festival float museum, and Sanmachi — the historic district where beautifully preserved old buildings line the streets. A knowledgeable local guide brings the history, culture and daily life of Takayama's past and present vividly to life.

  ## Highlights

  - Miyagawa Morning Market (宮川朝市) — fresh produce, local crafts and a taste of everyday Takayama life
  - Kusakabe Folk Museum or Yoshijima Heritage House — preserved traditional merchant houses with outstanding local architecture and woodwork
  - Takayama Festival Float Exhibition Hall — marvel at the intricately decorated floats used in Takayama's famous festivals
  - Sanmachi — the historic old town district where rows of traditional dark-timber buildings have been preserved for centuries

  ## FAQ

  **What happens if it rains?**
  This is a walking tour through covered market areas and indoor sites, so light rain is generally not a problem. We recommend bringing a compact umbrella or light rain jacket.

  **What should I wear?**
  Comfortable walking shoes are essential. Dress for the season — Takayama can be cold in spring and autumn mornings.

  **How much are entrance fees?**
  Entrance fees vary by site and are not included in the group tour price. Your guide will advise you on costs. Entrance fees are included for private tours.

  **Can I join if I am travelling solo?**
  Solo travellers are welcome to join a group tour (subject to availability). Please note the minimum group size is 2 for booking purposes — we will match you with other guests where possible.

  **Is there time for shopping?**
  Yes — the morning market and Sanmachi district both offer plenty of opportunity to browse local crafts, sake, sweets and souvenirs. Your guide can recommend the best spots.

  **How much are entrance fees?**
  Entrance fees vary by site and are not included in the group tour price. Your guide will advise on exact costs during the tour. Entrance fees are included for private tour guests.

  **Do I need to bring cash? If so, how much?**
  Some smaller shops and market stalls in Takayama only accept cash. We recommend bringing at least ¥3,000–¥5,000 in cash to cover entrance fees, any purchases, and extra snacks or drinks.
