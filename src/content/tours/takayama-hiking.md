  ## About This Tour

  Japan is rightly celebrated for its traditional yet modern urban culture found in cities like Tokyo and Kyoto. Takayama offers a different side of Japan — surrounded by towering mountains with a creative use of the limited land available. This tour lets you enjoy sweeping views across the town and the Hida mountain range, and on a clear day as far as the Northern Alps.

  After visiting traditional castle ruins, you'll explore the relationship between the rice-farming landscape and traditional rural architecture — especially the thatched-roof farmhouses of the Hida Folk Village.

  ## Highlights

  - Climb Matsukurayama for a panoramic view of Takayama and the surrounding peaks
  - Visit Matsukura Castle ruins — a historic site
  - Walk alongside rice paddies and explore the deep connection between farming and daily life
  - Tour the Hida Folk Village (飛騨民俗村) — traditional farmhouses preserved as they were centuries ago
  - Tea break at a local café

  ## FAQ

  **What happens if it rains?**
  If conditions are not too severe, the hiking tour goes ahead as planned. Your safety is our top priority — if we judge conditions to be unsafe, we will offer an alternative or provide a full refund.

  **What equipment should I bring?**
  Hiking boots or sturdy shoes, rain gear, at least 1 litre of water, a light snack, hat, and sunscreen. A camera is also recommended.

  **How much are entrance fees and transportation?**
  Transportation and entrance fees are not included in the tour price. Your guide will advise on costs during the tour.

  **What fitness level is required?**
  The hike involves moderate uphill walking. A reasonable level of fitness is recommended. Please let us know about any health concerns when booking.
